Ingredients

  • Garlic cloves (2)
  • Onion (small)
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Spaghetti pasta
  • Olive oil
  • Basil (fresh or dried)

Steps

  1. Prepare garlic and onion: chop garlic cloves, dice the onion.
  2. Boil water. Add pasta to boliling water and boil it as long as it is described on the bag.
  3. Heat one large pan with olive oil
  4. Put chopped garlic on the hot pan for up to 10 seconds. Then add onion.
  5. Add tomatoes.
  6. Add basil, salt and pepper
